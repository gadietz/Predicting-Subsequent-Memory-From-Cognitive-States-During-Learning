they were excluded from further study of the participant models.

to see if there was strong evidence that the logistic regression
model accuracy was higher than random chance (50% accuracy)

Sample Size              : 77

Since the P-Value is less than 0.05, we can say these results
are significant, and therefore there is strong evidence that our
modeling technique predicts memory accuracy.
